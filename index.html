About
Glam of World

*Glam of World Entertainment* was established in *2015* in Jaipur with a mission to create groundbreaking events that inspire, empower, and celebrate the heroes and stories that shape our world. Over the years, we’ve evolved into a leading event management company with a reputation for delivering high-quality, impactful, and memorable experiences.

From recognizing the accomplishments of women and underrepresented communities to honoring the real-life heroes in our society, *Glam of World Entertainment* continues to set the standard for excellence in the Indian events industry.

Brand Identity

Nari Tujhe Salam

A hallmark of our journey, **Nari Tujhe Salam** was an empowering event series held from **2015 to 2019** across major cities including **Jaipur, Kolkata, Chandigarh, and Gurugram**. This event served as a tribute to women across India, acknowledging their strength, resilience, and invaluable contributions to society. With each year, **Nari Tujhe Salam** grew in scale and significance, reaching thousands of attendees and impacting communities nationwide.

**Ek Samman Kinnaron Ke Naam (2018):**

In **2018**, **Glam of World Entertainment** achieved a historic milestone with **Ek Samman Kinnaron Ke Naam**, held at the **JECC Jaipur**. This pioneering event celebrated the **transgender community** for the first time in India, shining a light on their struggles and triumphs. It marked a significant moment in the country’s history for inclusivity, and we were proud to be at the forefront of such an important social movement.

02

**Ek Samman Kinnaron Ke Naam (2018):**

Ek Samman Kinnaron Ke Naam (2018) is a heartwarming tribute to the Kinnar community, celebrating their courage and resilience. This initiative recognizes their contributions to society, fostering inclusion and respect. It stands as a symbol of dignity, shedding light on the importance of equality for all.

**Khakee – A Real Hero for Police (2024):**

Our latest initiative, **Khakee: A Real Hero for Police**, is our largest and most ambitious event to date, celebrating the courage and dedication of India’s police force. With an audience of over **5 million+** people across India, this event highlights the unsung heroes who serve and protect our communities, showcasing their bravery and sacrifice. The response has been overwhelming, cementing **Glam of World Entertainment** as a platform for celebrating those who make a difference in society.

Khakee – A Real Hero for Police

A Real Hero for Police honors the unwavering dedication and bravery of the police force. This initiative celebrates their selfless service, safeguarding the community with courage. It highlights the sacrifices made by officers, shining a light on their role as real-life heroes.
